The things a quiz can't measure

Some of the most exacting people in the city are both — an apartment off Corso Magenta, padel on Saturday, and the spice rack alphabetized at midnight. What they share is a refusal to let a crooked frame, a missed step, or a slightly-off fatturato go unaddressed. If that sounds familiar, the line between taste and compulsion may be thinner than your cashmere.
